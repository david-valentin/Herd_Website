About Herd

In a small dingy garage in Palo Alto...Nope. The idea was based off a friend's github project. After doing some thinking on a cheap blow-up couch from Amazon, we thought the project could be expanded.

We wanted to build an app that gives you control the content you view and removes the excess noise of social media platforms: your Herd.

Herd's Mission

Bringing your closest friends together in the most seamless way possible.

You're best friends are uncoordinated, slow, and lazy but thats why they're your friends. Herd allows you to get together quickly and easily. Pick up basketball, set a waypoint, done. Your friends now know the how, where, and when. It's that simple.

Why Herd?

Have you been on Facebook mobile app? Yeah, thats what I thought.

Our social mobile app blends the social with functionality. Members of a Herd share their location data with their closest friends in real time, allowing them to meet up and get togethers on the fly. Being apart of a Herd is special and important.
